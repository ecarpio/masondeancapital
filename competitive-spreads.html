    Building Relationships

    Looking ahead to the future.

    Mason Dean Capital (MDC) is an 'Independent' Introducing Broker which means that they are able to maintain one or more relationships with selected dealing firms. In addition, this allows Mason Dean Capital to offer the customer a vast choice of trading platforms.

    When an MDC customer places a trade, it is passed through to proprietary software which aggregates the best bid/offer quote from over a hundred contributors including Top Tier 1 banks. Due to the level of sophistication in the aggregation software and access to deep liquidity, the customer trade is able to be executed seamlessly within a very narrow spread.
